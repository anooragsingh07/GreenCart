About GreenCart

The main problem we are solving is the lack of accessible and clear information about sustainable retail choices for everyday consumers. Many shoppers want to make eco-friendly and responsible purchasing decisions but struggle to find trustworthy details about product origins, environmental impacts, and the sustainability practices of different brands. This information gap leads to uninformed purchases that can contribute to waste, pollution, and unethical supply chains.

This problem occurs primarily in online and physical retail environments worldwide, especially as consumers become more aware of environmental issues but are overwhelmed by complex or scattered data. Without a simple, easy-to-understand resource that educates and guides them, consumers often default to convenience or price rather than sustainability. This gap limits the positive impact that conscious consumer behavior can have on reducing waste, lowering carbon footprints, and supporting ethical production practices.
